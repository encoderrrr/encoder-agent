How It Works

Simple, agentic, and payment-backed.

01

Request a service

Send a direct message to @encoderagent with a supported service request.

02

Receive a quote

The agent returns a quote and a job id so the work can be tracked end to end.

03

Pay on Unicity

A payment request is issued through Unicity. Once paid, the job continues automatically.

04

Get the result

The final result is delivered back over direct messages and the job status moves to completed.
